# 🔥💪 Food Suggestions - Quick Start Guide

## What's New?

A beautiful new **"🔥💪 Suggestions"** button on the Add Meal page that shows a curated database of 38 common foods sorted by protein content!

## How to Use It

### Step 1: Go to Add Meal Page
- Click "Add Meal" in the navigation bar

### Step 2: Click Suggestions Button
- Look for the **"🔥💪 Suggestions"** button at the top
- Three buttons available:
  - 🔥💪 Suggestions (NEW!)
  - 📋 Food Database (existing)
  - ✏️ Custom Meal (existing)

### Step 3: Browse Foods
- Modal opens showing all 38 foods
- Foods are pre-sorted by **protein content** (highest first)
- Look for 🥇 **HIGH PROTEIN** badge on foods with protein > 10g

### Step 4: Search (Optional)
- Type in the search box to filter foods
- Examples: "chicken", "egg", "rice", "protein"
- Search is case-insensitive and instant

### Step 5: Add Food
- Click **"Add"** button on any food card
- Form auto-fills with:
  - Food name
  - Calories
  - Protein
  - Carbs
  - Fats
- Modal closes automatically

### Step 6: Submit or Modify
- Modify any values if needed
- Click "Add Meal" to log it
- Or go back and add another food

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Find High-Protein Options
- Foods with 🥇 **HIGH PROTEIN** badge are ideal for muscle building
- Sorted by protein, so best options are at the top
- Examples: Protein Shake (24g), Chicken (31g), Egg Whites (11g)

### Quick Search Examples
- "protein" → Shows Protein Shake, Greek Yogurt, Egg Whites
- "chicken" → Shows Chicken Breast
- "egg" → Shows Egg, Egg Whites
- "rice" → Shows Rice, Brown Rice
- "dal" → Shows Dal
- "pizza" → Shows Pizza Slice

### For Muscle Building
- Focus on 🥇 foods (protein > 10g)
- Combine proteins with carbs
- Examples: Chicken + Rice, Paneer + Roti, Greek Yogurt + Oats

### For Weight Loss
- Look at calorie content
- Combine low-cal proteins: Egg Whites (51 cal), Tofu (144 cal)
- Add vegetables: Potato (87 cal), Sweet Potato (86 cal)

### For Balanced Meal
- Pick a protein (e.g., Chicken Breast - 165 cal, 31g protein)
- Add a carb (e.g., Brown Rice - 111 cal, 2.6g protein)
- Add a fat (e.g., Almonds - 70 cal, 6g fat)

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Why are foods sorted by protein?**
A: Most users track protein for muscle building and satiety. Highest protein foods are most useful, so they appear first!

**Q: Can I search while the modal is open?**
A: Yes! The search box is sticky and always visible. Type to filter instantly.

**Q: What if I can't find a food?**
A: 
1. Try searching with a simpler name
2. Use "📋 Food Database" option
3. Use "✏️ Custom Meal" to add custom values

**Q: Does the search work for partial names?**
A: Yes! Search "chick" to find "Chicken Breast"

**Q: Are the nutritional values accurate?**
A: Values are based on standard nutritional databases. For precise tracking, check food labels.

**Q: Can I add my own foods to the suggestions?**
A: Currently, the database is fixed. You can use "✏️ Custom Meal" to add foods manually.

**Q: Why do some foods have a gold badge?**
A: The 🥇 badge means the food has more than 10g of protein, making it excellent for protein goals.

**Q: How often is the database updated?**
A: The database loads once when you start the app. Refresh to get updates.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**Issue**: "Suggestions button not working"
- **Solution**: Reload the page, ensure food database loaded (check console)

**Issue**: "No foods showing in modal"
- **Solution**: Check console (F12) for errors, verify foods.csv exists

**Issue**: "Search not filtering"
- **Solution**: Verify you're typing in the search box, try pressing Escape and reopening

**Issue**: "Add button not filling form"
- **Solution**: Close modal and refresh page, try again
